# Brief: buyer accounts

Source: client proposal (Marlowe & Hart)

Right now anyone can browse listings and send an inquiry, but there's nothing that keeps a buyer coming back. People email us asking "which ones did I message again?" and we have no good answer. We want buyers to have their own little space.

What we're after:
- Buyers can create an account and sign in. (Not sure yet if we want the usual email + password like the admin side, or one of those magic-link / Google sign-in things. Your call, tell us what's simplest.)
- Once signed in they can save listings they like to a shortlist, and take them off again. A little heart or save button on the listing, basically.
- One place where they can see the inquiries they've already sent us, so they stop losing track.

A few things we haven't decided:
- Should the shortlist be totally private to each buyer, or do we eventually want them to share it (send a link to a partner, that kind of thing)? Leaning private for now but flag it.
- What happens to inquiries someone sent before they made an account? Nice to have them show up but not a dealbreaker.

Keep the admin side exactly as it is. This is buyer-facing only. Nothing here should let a buyer near the admin dashboard or anyone else's data.
